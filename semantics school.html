Facts about Govt. Azizul hoque College

Government Azizul Haque College,also known as AHC or A. H. College, is a public college in Bogura district of Bangladesh. It is one of the largest and top-ranked educational institutions in northern Bangladesh. It was established in 1939 as a Higher Secondary college and named after Sir Azizul Haque, the then Vice-chancellor of Calcutta University

The college is presently affiliated with the National University of Bangladesh and offers Bachelor's degree (Pass), Bachelor's degree (Honours), Master's Preliminary and Master's Final courses in various disciplines. It is also a Study Centre for the MBA program affiliated with Bangladesh Open University. In the 'National University College Ranking 2018', AHC was ranked 3rd among all the affiliated colleges of National University.
